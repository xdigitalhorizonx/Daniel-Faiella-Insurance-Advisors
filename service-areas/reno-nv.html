Medicare, locally

Medicare in Reno & Washoe County.

Reno’s big draw is choice. Several Medicare Advantage carriers compete here, including Senior Care Plus (the plan tied to Renown’s Hometown Health) and Prominence, alongside the national names. More competition can mean richer extra benefits — but only if the plan’s network includes your providers.

The Renown vs. Saint Mary’s question matters more in Reno than anywhere else in the region. Senior Care Plus is built around the Renown/Hometown Health network; if your specialists are at Saint Mary’s or Northern Nevada Medical Center, a different plan may fit better. We check before you commit, not after.

Prefer to keep every door open? A Medicare Supplement paired with a standalone Part D plan lets you see any Reno provider who takes Medicare, with predictable costs. We’ll compare that against Advantage plans in real dollars.

New to Reno? Retirees relocating from California and elsewhere are a big part of my week. If you moved here, that triggers a special window to switch to a Nevada plan — don’t assume your old plan travels.

Plan availability and provider networks change every year and by ZIP code. I don’t offer every plan in the area — we’ll review the ones that fit your doctors and budget, and you can always compare all options at Medicare.gov or 1-800-MEDICARE.

Good questions

Frequently asked.

Which Medicare plans work with Renown or Saint Mary’s in Reno?

It varies by plan and by year. Senior Care Plus is built around the Renown/Hometown Health network, while other Advantage plans and all Medicare Supplement plans handle the Reno hospital systems differently. Tell me which doctors and hospital you use and I’ll show you the plans that actually cover them.

I just moved to Reno. Do I need to change my Medicare plan?

Probably. Medicare Advantage and Part D plans are regional, so a plan from another state or county usually won’t serve you well here. Moving triggers a Special Enrollment Period, and I’ll help you pick a Reno-area plan during it.

Do you cover Sparks and Spanish Springs too?

Yes — all of Washoe County and the greater Truckee Meadows. I regularly meet clients in Sparks, Spanish Springs, Sun Valley, and the Northwest, or by video anywhere.

Is your help really free?

Yes. I’m an independent broker paid by the carrier you choose, so a full comparison and enrollment costs you nothing and your premium doesn’t change. You just get local, unbiased help instead of a 1-800 number.
