## Scope & honesty note (read this first)

This analysis is **grounded in live July-2026 SERP research** — ~40 real searches across the
five markets, reverse-engineering who ranks and how (their URLs, title tags, content clusters,
review counts, and the directories that dominate). Search-result titles *are* competitors'
real `<title>` tags, so title/URL/tactic data is observed, not guessed.

**What it does NOT contain, and why:** hard **monthly search volume, keyword difficulty (KD),
CPC, or backlink/Domain-Authority numbers.** Those require a paid tool (Semrush / Ahrefs /
Google Keyword Planner) that isn't available in this environment. Priorities below are therefore
**reasoned from SERP evidence** (how contested a term is, intent value, market softness) — a
defensible qualitative model, not a volume table. The tables include **empty `Volume` / `KD`
columns** so you can paste real numbers from your tool and re-sort. See §5.

Items marked *(inferred)* could not be HTML-confirmed (the research environment couldn't fetch
competitors' raw HTML); treat them as hypotheses to verify.
