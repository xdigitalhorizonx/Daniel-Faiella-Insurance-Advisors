the advisor who teaches first

Independent, local, and on your side.

Most people meet an insurance agent when they’re being sold something. I built my practice to be the opposite of that. My job is to teach you how your options actually work — Medicare, life, long-term care, and retirement income — so you make the call, not me.

As an independent broker, I’m not tied to a single company. I shop A+ rated carriers and bring you the ones that fit your doctors, your budget, and your plans — never just one insurer’s menu. And because carriers pay the broker, my help costs you nothing; your premium is identical whether you use me or not.

I’m based in Carson City and serve all of Northern Nevada — Reno and Sparks, the Carson Valley towns of Minden and Gardnerville, Dayton and Lyon County, and the Nevada side of Lake Tahoe. We meet wherever works for you: your kitchen table, a local coffee shop, or a video call. It’s just me — no call center, no script.

How I work

Three promises.

1 · Teach first

We start with how your options work, in plain language, before we ever talk about a specific plan. You’ll understand your coverage well enough to explain it to a friend.

2 · Shop it honestly

I compare A+ rated carriers side by side and recommend what fits you — not what pays the most. If the best answer is to keep what you have, I’ll tell you that.

3 · Stay your agent

The same person who sets your plan up answers the phone next year when something changes. Long after enrollment, I’m still here.

What I help with

Two halves of one plan — under one roof.

Protect your health and your time, and protect the money that funds whatever is next. As an independent broker I shop A+ rated carriers for you — never just one company’s menu.
