Good questions

Frequently asked.

How much does it cost to work with Daniel Faiella?

Nothing. Daniel is an independent broker paid by the insurance carrier you choose, so consultations, comparisons, and enrollment are free — and your premium is the same whether you use him or not.

What areas does Daniel Faiella Insurance Advisors serve?

Carson City is home base, with service throughout nearby Northern Nevada — including Reno, Sparks, Minden, Gardnerville, Incline Village, and Dayton.

Are you affiliated with Medicare or the government?

No. Daniel is an independent licensed insurance agent and is not connected with or endorsed by the U.S. government, the federal Medicare program, or any government agency.

What kinds of insurance do you help with?

Medicare (Advantage, Supplement/Medigap and Part D), health and hospital indemnity, life insurance, long-term care, retirement income through fixed and indexed annuities, and insurance-based estate and legacy planning.
